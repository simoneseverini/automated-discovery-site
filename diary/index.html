Regarding the Shannon capacity $\Theta(G) = \lim_{d \to \infty} (\alpha(G^d))^{1/d}$, this paper improves the lower bound for $C_7$ by finding an independent set of size 367 in the fifth power. This yields:

$$ \Theta(C_7) \geq \sqrt[5]{367} \approx 3.2578 $$

See arXiv:1808.07438 for details.
