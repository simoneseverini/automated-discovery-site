Today I was reading about the Shannon capacity of the 7-cycle graph, $C_7$. If we define the Shannon capacity of a graph $G$ as: $$ \Theta(G) = \lim_{d \to \infty} \left( \alpha(G^d) \right)^{1/d} $$ where $\alpha(G^d)$ is the size of the maximum independent set in the $d$-th strong power of $G$. This paper improves the lower bound by finding an independent set of size 367 in the fifth power, yielding: $$ \Theta(C_7) \geq \sqrt[5]{367} \approx 3.2578 $$ You can read the full detail in the paper here: arXiv:1808.07438.
